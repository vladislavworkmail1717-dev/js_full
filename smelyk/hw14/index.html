We do the research so you can donate with confidence to thoroughly vetted, high-impact organizations.

Through rigorous prioritization and evaluation, The Life You Can Save identifies the most effective giving opportunities based on impact, cost-effectiveness, transparency, track record and ability to use additional funding productively. Our methodology examines multidimensional poverty, looking beyond income to include health, education, and living standards to ensure we’re addressing the root causes of extreme poverty.

You want to know your donations are actually reaching people in need, which is why we do the work to make it easy for you to identify where your gifts will be most impactful. We look at the data to determine the best interventions across the many dimensions of poverty. Then we identify the highest-impact charities delivering those interventions, ensuring that we are helping the most in need, in the best known ways possible.
